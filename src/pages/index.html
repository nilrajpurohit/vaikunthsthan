OUR SERVICES
Antyeshti Samagri
(funeral rites materials)
As per religion and caste, all the items will be provided to the family of deceased. No need to carry a single thing.
Vimukti Vahan
(Ambulance/Hearse)
We will help in shifting the Deceased from Hospital to Home and later to the Crematorium for the final rites. The vehicle will be provided as per the need (decoration of vehicle is also provided)
Sevak
(Manpower)
Our people will be with you from the beginning till end to ensure smooth functioning of the entire process.
Registration at Cemetery & Death Certificate
Registration at cemetery will be taken care by us . and with death certificate process will be also taken care by us only.
Bhataji/Pandit 10th,12th,13th Shradha Vidhi
Bhataji / Pandit/ Jangam will be provided as the religion and caste at the said place and time. They will be with you the entire process.
Immersion of Asthi

Asthi Immersion (Asthi Visarjan), The entire of immersion of asthi will be taken care by us .
Dead Body Freezer/Dead Body Dispatch/Embalming
We provide a Dead Body Freezer as per convenience of the family at their place. All the arrange will carry out by our manpower. So the family need not worry about it.
Co-Ordination for Organ Donation
Organ donation is greatest gift post death. We will help if the deceased or the family of deceased wish to do so.
Arrangement for Condolence Meeting
Arrangment of condolence meeting for the deceased we will done by us. Message by acknowledging the loss and that you were deeply sorry to learn of the news
